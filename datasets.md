# Curated Datasets for Automation Research

**Version**: 1.0 | **Date**: October 25, 2025

---

This document provides a curated list of key datasets that are widely used in automation research, particularly in the fields of computer vision and robotics.

## Computer Vision Datasets

### 1. ImageNet

*   **Description:** A large-scale dataset of over 14 million images, hand-annotated to indicate what objects are pictured. The images are organized into a hierarchy, with each node of the hierarchy corresponding to a concept in WordNet.
*   **Size:** 14 million images, 20,000+ categories.
*   **Task(s):** Image classification, object localization, object detection.
*   **Relevance:** The ImageNet Large Scale Visual Recognition Challenge (ILSVRC) has been a key benchmark for the development of deep learning models for computer vision.

### 2. COCO (Common Objects in Context)

*   **Description:** A large-scale object detection, segmentation, and captioning dataset. COCO is designed to be more representative of real-world scenes, with objects appearing in their natural context.
*   **Size:** 330K images, 1.5 million object instances, 80 object categories.
*   **Task(s):** Object detection, segmentation, image captioning.
*   **Relevance:** COCO is the standard benchmark for object detection and segmentation models.

## Robotics and Autonomous Driving Datasets

### 3. RoboNet

*   **Description:** A large-scale dataset of robot interaction videos. The dataset contains over 15 million video frames from a variety of different robots and environments.
*   **Size:** 15 million video frames.
*   **Task(s):** Learning robot dynamics, imitation learning, reinforcement learning.
*   **Relevance:** RoboNet is a key dataset for research in robot learning.

### 4. nuScenes

*   **Description:** A large-scale public dataset for autonomous driving. The dataset was collected in Boston and Singapore, and it includes data from a full sensor suite, including 6 cameras, 1 LiDAR, 5 radar, GPS, and IMU.
*   **Size:** 1000 scenes, each 20 seconds long.
*   **Task(s):** 3D object detection, tracking, and prediction.
*   **Relevance:** nuScenes is a key benchmark for the development of autonomous driving systems.

### 5. KITTI

*   **Description:** One of the most popular datasets for autonomous driving research. The dataset was collected in and around the city of Karlsruhe, Germany, and it includes data from a variety of sensors, including cameras, LiDAR, and GPS/IMU.
*   **Size:** 6 hours of traffic scenarios.
*   **Task(s):** Stereo vision, optical flow, visual odometry, 3D object detection, and 3D tracking.
*   **Relevance:** The KITTI benchmark has been instrumental in advancing the state-of-the-art in autonomous driving research.
